## Overview
The Imagination Field Simulator is a symbolic visualization system that represents
internal perception, imagination, and energy flow through dynamic visual elements.

This project is **conceptual and artistic**, not scientific or medical.

---

## Core Elements

### 1. Central Iris
The iris represents the point of focus or awareness.
Its size and glow respond to the symbolic energy level.

### 2. Particle Field
Particles represent flowing thoughts or imaginative fragments.
Higher energy levels increase particle density and motion.

### 3. Energy Axis
The energy slider acts as a symbolic internal parameter,
influencing complexity and intensity of the field.

### 4. Imagination Scan Mode
When activated, higher-order fractal patterns appear,
symbolizing deeper imagination or creative activation.
